# 🔐 SafeVault – Emergency Security Locker

## 📌 Project Name
SafeVault

SafeVault is a full stack MERN application designed to securely store a user's important emergency information in one protected digital locker.

The platform allows users to store and manage critical life information such as medical details, emergency contacts, identity records, insurance details, and personal notes so that this information can be accessed quickly during emergencies.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

During emergencies like accidents, medical issues, or sudden family situations, people often fail to access important information at the right time.

Important details such as:

- Blood group
- Emergency contacts
- Insurance policy numbers
- Identity documents
- Medical conditions
- Hospital preferences

are usually scattered across phones, emails, papers, or memory.

This creates confusion and delays during critical situations.

There is no single secure place where all emergency information can be stored and accessed easily.

---

## 💡 Solution

SafeVault solves this problem by providing a secure digital locker where users can safely store all important emergency information in one place.

Users can manage their personal emergency data and quickly access it whenever needed.

The system keeps information organized, secure, and easy to retrieve during critical situations.

---

## ✨ Features

- User Signup and Login Authentication  
- Secure Emergency Profile Management  
- Emergency Contacts Storage  
- Important Documents Information Locker  
- Medical Information Storage (blood group, allergies, conditions)  
- Trusted Contacts System  
- Full CRUD Operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete)  
- Search and Filter functionality  
- Dark Mode / Light Mode  
- Responsive UI for Mobile and Desktop

## ⭐ Importance of SafeVault

SafeVault is designed to solve a real-world problem.

During emergencies people often ask questions like:

- What is the patient's blood group?
- Who should be contacted first?
- Where are the insurance details?
- Which hospital should we go to?

SafeVault keeps all this information in one secure place.

It helps families, doctors, and trusted contacts quickly access important information during emergencies, potentially saving valuable time and improving response in critical situations.
